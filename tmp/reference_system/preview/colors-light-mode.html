Light mode

Cream · Sage · Pastel tints

Light mode anchors on a warm cream page (#F8F5EC) with pure white cards lifted by soft shadows and hairline borders. Sage replaces yellow as the headline accent — yellow is reserved for primary buttons, the bow on the mascot, and focus borders. Status surfaces use four high-key pastel tints: mint, butter, lilac, sky.

Surface scale

Six steps from the cream page background up to the warm-gray muted panel. Cards are pure white — they LIFT off the cream page rather than recess into it.

Tinted status surfaces

Pastel tints used on dashboard kanban columns, status chips, "Available" rows, and inline callouts. Each has a paired on-tint ink (--sk-on-mint etc.) for accessible labels.

Done · Available Mint Completed work, "available" artifact rows, success states. --sk-tint-mint · #E8F4E8
Doing · Next up Butter Active "Next:" callouts, in-progress columns, attention-needed rows. --sk-tint-butter · #FFF6D9
For review · v2.x Lilac Review states, version 2.x evolution accents, the Constitution chip. --sk-tint-lilac · #F0EAF8
Info · Doctrine Sky Information panels, doctrine & knowledge banners, neutral info. --sk-tint-sky · #E4EDF8
